Pranadhara is an Indian brand that specialises in water purification products designed to increase the pH levels of drinking water. Their primary focus is on alkalising and mineralising regular water to support hydration and body acidity balance. 
According to the manufacturer and general alkaline water proponents, these products may provide:
Better Hydration: By breaking water into smaller particles for easier cellular absorption.
Acidity Neutralisation: Helps balance pH levels disrupted by modern acidic diets.
Antioxidant Support: Higher pH levels and negative ORP may help fight free radicals.
Improved Digestion: May assist with mild acidity or acid reflux symptoms.
